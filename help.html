How do I get a summary of the webpage I'm looking at?

Click on the reading assistant button that appears on the webpage, then select "Summary" and a summary of the entire article/page will appear after a few seconds

How does the summary/quiz work?

Using the gpt-3.5-turbo-0125 API, the website's writing is combined with some careful prompt engineering to create the output you see on the quiz and summary pages

How do I change the color of the website?

Click on the down arrow underneath the "Reading Assistant" button on the webpage. Click on the color palette icon, then select the color you want from the dropdown menu
